Welcome to Daniel's Humane Society

Daniel Loves The Oregon Humane Society who are responsible for rescues, heals and adopts more than 11,000 pets each year. They never place a time limit on how long cats, dogs and other pets stay at our shelter. OHS relies on donations to support our adoption, education, and animal rescue programs.
